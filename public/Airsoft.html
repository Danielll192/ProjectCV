Airsoft

what is airsoft?

Airsoft is a team-based shooting game in which participants eliminate opposing players out of play by shooting them with spherical plastic projectiles (BB-s) shot from airsoft guns.

The Airsoft guns are made 100% to look like real guns, with all accessories, what you can see on the images as well, such as the airsoft gears like helmets and vests.
