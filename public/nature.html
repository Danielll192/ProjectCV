Walk in the nature

Nature is a charger for my soul, if my phone haves low battery i put it on a charger, when my body and soul is tired, i go to the nature to charge it.

I love walk in the nature with my girlfriend, named Tina, we were in the High Tatras last year, they are really really beautiful mountains in Slovakia. We were there in November for 5 days, unfortunately it was mostly foggy and the we didn't see the mountains but when the fog lifted, it was incredebly to see how beautiful the nature is. We decided to go back this year in summer to see how the mountains looks in summer sunshine and to hike in mountain routes.
